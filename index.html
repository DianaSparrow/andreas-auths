Do not submit Wegovy for this patient
NY Medicaid does not cover Wegovy as a weight loss medication for patients with Type 2 diabetes. A submission is very likely to be denied.
Consider prescribing one of these instead
For patients with Type 2 diabetes on NY Medicaid, approval is much more likely if the Rx is written for a GLP-1 or dual agonist under the diabetes indication rather than the obesity indication.
Mounjaro (tirzepatide)
Dual GIP/GLP-1 agonist approved for Type 2 diabetes. Same molecule as Zepbound but covered under the diabetes indication — significantly easier to approve on Medicaid.
Victoza (liraglutide)
Diabetes-indication version of liraglutide, covered under NY Medicaid for Type 2 diabetes patients. Note: Saxenda is the obesity indication and much harder to approve.
Ozempic (semaglutide)
Diabetes-indication version of semaglutide — same molecule as Wegovy — and significantly more likely to be approved on NY Medicaid for a Type 2 diabetes patient.
